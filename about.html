Our Story


Giftz is a famous gift shop located in the center of Sri Lanka, established at the age of 25 by a young creative entrepreneur. With the conviction that presents are tokens of love and connection, Giftz is dedicated to bringing happiness and enduring memories to life via well chosen gifts

At Giftz, we handpick a wide variety of products and provide personalized alternatives so that every present is as special as the memory that goes along with it. We are aware of the ability of a thoughtful gift to deepen relationships and make any occasion special

We consider ourselves as partners in spreading happiness, one present at a time, and our mission extends beyond simply operating a gift shop. Our goal is to make the process of giving gifts easy and enjoyable for you by placing a strong emphasis on originality and quality

Come along on this journey of fostering relationships, creating connections, and elevating every event with Giftz. Because, at Giftz, we consider gifting to be a language of love.
